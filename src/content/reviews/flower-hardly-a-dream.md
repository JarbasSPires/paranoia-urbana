Que banda foda, meus caros! Sinto como se estivesse ouvindo Nausea pela
primeira vez. O ambiente e a estética, a Profane Existence nos apresentando

Se você curte Nausea, Sacrilege, Antischism, prepare-se, porque este álbum

incríveis, um crust punk com aquela pitada de metal, simplesmente
sensacional.

Que safra de bandas boas temos com Flower e Hope, não é mesmo? Elas estão no

<div data-lang-content="en">

What a fucking great band, my friends! I feel like I'm hearing Nausea for
the first time. The atmosphere and aesthetic — Profane Existence
introducing us to this incredible band — is simply amazing. There are no
words to describe it.

If you're into Nausea, Sacrilege, Antischism, get ready, because this
album is going to move you. You can't help but listen to the whole of
*Hardly a Dream*; it's one banger after another. Tracks like "Lethargy"
and "Retribution" are incredible, crust punk with that touch of metal,
simply sensational.

What a crop of great bands we have with Flower and Hope, right? They're
at the top of these wonderful bands that are emerging. And may many more
come! Incredible, simply incredible!
